5V5 BASKETBALL

Full court. Full team. Full intensity.

WHAT IS 5V5?

5v5 basketball is the official full-court version of the game. Two teams of five compete with strategy, teamwork, and coordination.

TEAM ROLES

Point Guard (PG)

Leads the offense and controls the pace.

Shooting Guard (SG)

Primary scorer and perimeter shooter.

Small Forward (SF)

Balanced scorer and defender.

Power Forward (PF)

Strong inside presence and rebounder.

Center (C)

Protects the rim and dominates the paint.

GAME STYLE

5v5 basketball is about teamwork. Every pass, every screen, every rotation matters. One mistake affects the entire team.

ARENA ENERGY

Where teamwork creates champions.

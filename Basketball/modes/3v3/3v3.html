3V3 BASKETBALL

Fast pace. Street style. Pure creativity.

WHAT IS 3V3?

3v3 basketball is played on a half-court with three players per team. It’s faster, more open, and focuses on quick decisions and teamwork.

WHY PLAY 3V3?

Speed

Less players means faster gameplay and constant action.

Creativity

More space allows players to express their unique style.

Team Chemistry

Every player matters — strong connection wins games.

PLAYSTYLE

3v3 is about quick movement, spacing, and smart plays. Every second counts, and every decision changes the game.

STREET CULTURE

3v3 basketball represents freedom. It’s where players show personality, creativity, and confidence. It’s not just competition — it’s expression.

THE GAME NEVER STOPS

Fast breaks. Quick shots. Endless energy.
